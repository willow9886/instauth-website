Welcome to Instauth.

This is the home of Instauth, the instant single sign-on and two-factor authentication solution.

Our Mission

Making the world a more secure place by making it super easy to integrate identity solutions into your software stack.

Our Solution

Instauth allows any Juju-enabled indenty solution that implements the instauth relation to be instantly integrated with any other Juju-enabled solution in order to provide instant single sign-on and two-factor authentication. The instauth relation internally supports OpenID Connect and UMA.

Members

Current members of the instauth program include Gluu, Forgerock, WSO2, Keydap, Tirasa & SecureAuth. Membership is free.
